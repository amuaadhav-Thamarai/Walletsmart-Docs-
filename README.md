# WalletSmart Mobile Banking App

## User Onboarding Guide
Welcome to WalletSmart! This guide will help you set up your account and start managing your finances securely in just a few simple steps.

---

# Step 1: Download the App & Create Your Account

### 1.1 Download WalletSmart
1. Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
2. Search for **WalletSmart**.
3. Tap **Install** and wait for the download to complete.
4. Open the WalletSmart app.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: App Store/Google Play Store Download Page]*

### 1.2 Create Your Account
1. Tap **Get Started**.
2. Enter your mobile number or email address.
3. Create a strong password.
4. Read and accept the Terms & Conditions.
5. Tap **Create Account**.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Account Creation Screen]*

### 1.3 Verify Contact Information
1. Check your SMS or email for a verification code.
2. Enter the code in the app.
3. Tap **Verify** to continue.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Verification Code Screen]*

---

# Step 2: Verify Your Identity (KYC)
To comply with banking regulations and keep your account secure, WalletSmart requires identity verification.

### 2.1 Start KYC Verification
1. From the home screen, tap **Complete Verification**.
2. Select your country of residence.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: KYC Welcome Screen]*

### 2.2 Upload Identification Documents
1. Choose an accepted ID document (Passport, Driver's License, or National ID).
2. Take clear photos of the front and back of your ID.
3. Ensure all details are visible and readable.
4. Tap **Submit**.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: ID Upload Screen]*

### 2.3 Complete Face Verification
1. Follow the on-screen instructions to take a selfie.
2. Position your face within the camera frame.
3. Wait while the app verifies your identity.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Selfie Verification Screen]*

### 2.4 Verification Confirmation
1. Once submitted, your documents will be reviewed.
2. You will receive a notification when verification is complete.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: KYC Success Screen]*

# Step 3: Link a Bank Account
Linking a bank account allows you to transfer funds and manage transactions seamlessly.

### 3.1 Open Bank Linking
1. Log in to WalletSmart.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Linked Accounts**.
3. Tap **Add Bank Account**.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Linked Accounts Screen]*

### 3.2 Select Your Bank
1. Choose your bank from the list.
2. Tap **Continue**.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Bank Selection Screen]*

### 3.3 Connect Your Account
1. Enter the required banking credentials or account details.
2. Review the permissions requested by WalletSmart.
3. Tap **Authorize**.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Bank Authorization Screen]*

### 3.4 Confirm Successful Linking
1. Wait for the connection to complete.
2. Verify that your bank account appears in the Linked Accounts section.
3. Tap **Done**.

*\[Screenshot Placeholder: Bank Account Linked Successfully Screen]*

# You're All Set!
Congratulations! Your WalletSmart account is now ready to use. You can securely manage your finances, transfer money, and track transactions directly from the app.

For additional assistance, visit the Help Center or contact WalletSmart Customer Support.
